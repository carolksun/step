Work Experience

  • Google

    I am currently a STEP intern at Google, learning how to develop a website and later, contributing to open source project.

  • OpenX

    Last summer, I worked on the data science team at OpenX, an ad tech company, developing a machine learning model to predict the profitability of an ad request. I used Tensorflow and Google Cloud Platform to create a boosted tree model that could save the company 10% of its costs. This project is now working in production!

  • National Institues of Health

    As part of the Summer Internship Program at the National Institutes of Health, I used patient genotyping data to adjust protein expression to increase the performance of a Multiple Sclerosis diagonistic test. I also applied this method to improve the performance of a model that predicts the progression of the disease in patients.

Charts

I downloaded my sleep data from my Fitbit and plotted them as a line graph with a moving average. It's interesting to see how midterms/finals and other major events impact my sleep! The sleep score is a score from 0-100 and is "based on your heart rate, the time you spend awake or restless, and your sleep stages." * The deep sleep minutes is the time I spent in the deep sleep stage that night.
